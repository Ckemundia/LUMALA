Welcome to Lumala Logistics

Reliable logistics and freight solutions crafted for businesses of all sizes.

Brief History

Lumala Logistics was founded in 2014 as a small, family–owned courier company operating within one city. Over the years, we invested in modern trucks, cold–chain vehicles, air–freight partners and a digital tracking system to help businesses move goods safely and efficiently.

Today, Lumala Logistics connects warehouses, factories and customers across major cities and ports in Africa. Our dedicated operations team, trained drivers and advanced routing software allow us to deliver on time, every time.

Our latest rebrand and advertising campaigns, designed using professional tools such as Adobe Photoshop, have helped us communicate our promise of speed and safety to thousands of customers.

What We Offer

We provide end–to–end logistics services that cover collection, warehousing, packaging, customs clearance and last–mile delivery.

  • Road Freight

    Nationwide distribution using a fleet of modern trucks equipped with GPS tracking.

  • Air & Sea Freight

    International deliveries through our airline and shipping partners, including customs handling.

  • Warehousing & Fulfilment

    Secure storage, inventory management and order fulfilment for e‑commerce and retail businesses.
